Birth

Bill Gates was born in Seattle, Washington on October 28, 1955. He is the son of William H. Gates Sr and Mary Maxwell Gates .

Starting Microsoft

In 1975,Bill Gates and Paul Allen co-founded Microsoft, which became the world's largest PC software company. During his career at Microsoft, Gates held the positions of chairman, CEO and chief software architect

More about Gates

Gates is one of the best-known entrepreneurs of the personal computer revolution. He has been criticized for his business tactics, which have been considered anti-competitive

Success

Since 1987,Bill Gates has been included in the Forbes list of the world's wealthiest people.[12] As of May 2017, Gates is the richest person in the world, with an estimated net worth of US$88.5 billion.

Charity

In 2009,Bill Gates and Warren Buffett founded The Giving Pledge, whereby they and other billionaires pledge to give at least half of their wealth to philanthropy.

Presidential Medal of Freedom

2016

The Presidential Medal of Freedom is an award bestowed by the President of the United States and is—along with the comparable Congressional Gold Medal—the highest civilian award of the United States.

Silver Buffalo Award

2010

The Silver Buffalo Award is the national-level distinguished service award of the Boy Scouts of America. It is presented for noteworthy and extraordinary service to youth on a national basis.

Padma Bhushan

2015

The Padma Bhushan is the third-highest civilian award in the Republic of India, preceded by the Bharat Ratna and the Padma Vibhushan and followed by the Padma Shri.
